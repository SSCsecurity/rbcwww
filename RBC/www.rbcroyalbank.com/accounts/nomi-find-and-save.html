          NOMI Finds and You Save – It’s That Simple!

          Using predictive technology, NOMI Find & Savelegal bug 4 does all the work for you, so you can save money without lifting a finger.

          Get Started with NOMI

          Key Features

          • Predictive Technology
            NOMI Find & Save will learn your transaction patterns, find extra dollars that it thinks you won’t miss and set them aside for you automatically.
          • Free Transfers
            Transfers are free to and from any eligible RBC chequing or savings accountlegal bug 4.
          • Automatic Payment Protection
            We’ll automatically move money back to your linked chequing account to cover an upcoming payment, transfer or overdraft balance if a payment or transfer is due to come out of your linked chequing account or you have an overdraft balance in itlegal bug 1.

            It’s your money after all.

          New to NOMI Find & Save

          NOMI Find & Save has increased its automatic transfer limit from $50 to $75 per day.

          Since NOMI Find & Save uses predictive technology to understand your personalized transaction patterns and find money it thinks you won’t miss, you may not see any increase in your transfer amounts from your source chequing account. While the automatic transfer amount can’t be customized, it does make it possible for you to save even faster! You still have control over your account and can pause and resume the automatic transfers in NOMI Find & Save at any time.

          NOMI Find & Save – Frequently Asked Questions

          NOMI Find & Save is a digital savings account that understands your transaction patterns, finds extra dollars in your cash flow and automatically moves them to savings. Just like that, you’ve saved money you didn’t know you could! Turn on NOMI Find & Save in the RBC Mobile app, then see your balance, transaction history and statements through the RBC Mobile app or RBC Online Banking.

          The maximum amount NOMI Find & Save will automatically transfer from your eligible chequing account is $75 per day, up to 5 times per week.

          Access your money anytime by simply transferring money from your NOMI Find & Save account to any eligible chequing or savings account. You can do this for free within the RBC Mobile app or through RBC Online Banking. At this time, no other transaction types can be made from the NOMI Find & Save account (e.g. ATM withdrawal, point-of-sale Interac purchases, 3rd party debits or credits, etc.)

          Yes, can transfer to your NOMI Find & Save account from any eligible chequing or savings account.

          No, you cannot customize the amount; NOMI Find & Save’s predictive technology understands your transaction patterns to find how much to save for you. If you are expecting an unusual withdrawal, the pause & resume function is a great tool to use. Simply pause NOMI Find & Save and set a date for it to resume after the expected withdrawal.

          • If your source account has pending cheques or pre-authorized payments whose value exceeds the available balance, NOMI Find & Save will move money (depending on your balance) to cover the payments and reimburse fees incurred as a result of these transactions.
          • Transfers to the source account from the NOMI Find & Save account will occur regardless of whether the linked NOMI Find & Save account is solely owned or jointly owned, and regardless of the original source of funds in the NOMI Find & Save account.
          • This NOMI Find & Save service does not apply to any real-time transactions such as, Point of Sale purchases, ATM withdrawals, or Interac e-Transfer transactions.

          You can pause NOMI Find & Save transfers at any time within the RBC Mobile app or by speaking to an RBC Advisor. This feature allows you to temporarily pause NOMI Find & Save if you know you have an unusual withdrawal coming up, for example, a special anniversary trip or your child starting daycare. Go to NOMI Find & Save Settings in the RBC Mobile app and swipe the pause toggle button to the “On” position and enter the date you would like NOMI Find & Save transfers to resume. A message indicating that your account is paused will appear whenever you enter the NOMI Find & Save details page.
